How It Works

The challenge of adding resistance by using Zumba® Toning Sticks
(or light weights), helps you focus on specific muscle groups,
so you (and your muscles) stay engaged!

Benefits

Lightweight maraca-like Toning Sticks enhances sense of rhythm and
coordination, while toning target zones, including arms, core and
lower body.
